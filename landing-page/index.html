Powerful Features

Instant Explanations

Select any text on a webpage and get a clear, concise AI-generated explanation in seconds. No need to open a new tab or search manually.

Dive Deeper

Want more context? Click "Dive Deeper" to start a conversation. Ask follow-up questions and explore the topic without losing your place.

Multi-Popup Support

Privacy First

Your data stays yours. No analytics, no tracking, no background collection. Text is only sent when you explicitly click the button.

Streaming Responses

See AI responses appear word-by-word in real-time. No waiting for the complete response before you can start reading.

How It Works

Get started in three simple steps

1

Select Any Text

Highlight any term, phrase, or sentence on any webpage. A small button will appear near your selection.

2

Click the Button

Click the sparkle button to get an instant AI-generated explanation. The response streams in real-time so you can start reading immediately.

3

Dive Deeper (Optional)

Want to learn more? Click "Dive Deeper" to open a chat interface. Ask follow-up questions, request examples, or explore related concepts.
